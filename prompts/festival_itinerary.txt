Festival Itinerary Prompt (v1)
Goal: Generate a premium, concise, festival weekend plan.

Inputs:
- Festival name, city, dates attending
- Genres + must-see artists (if any)
- Travel style (chill vs high-energy)
- Budget (low/medium/high)
- Lodging area (if known)

Output:
- Day-by-day plan (Morning / Pre-festival / Festival / After)
- 3 food picks + 2 nightlife picks near the festival area
- Transportation plan (arrival + exit strategy)
- Packing essentials (10 items)
- 3 “avoid these mistakes” tips
Tone: premium, confident, practical.
